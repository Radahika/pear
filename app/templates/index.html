Simplify Life

Housemates takes the pain out of living with roomates by helping handle the more annoying parts of living together.

Finances

We keep track of dept so you don't have to. Just enter what you buy for the house and we will take care of notifying your roomates what they owe you. We'll even crunch the numbers.

Balance Work

Someone not helping around the house? Relax. Tell Housemates the chores and we will rotate through the roommates to keep track of whose turn it is.

Talk It Out

Housemates gives you a place to talk all things house related. We give you a centralized comment board to leave messages, as well as anonymous comments.

Track Bills

We can remind you when your bills are due so you can avoid getting the power turned off on you during your parties! We can even let your roommates know how much they owe you each month.

Privacy

Your room gets its own unique access code, ensuring that other users can't see your data.

Celebrate

Be a better roommate, and live with better people. We make it easy to focus on the good times by handling the dirty work.
